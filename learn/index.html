L20n by example

L20n is an open source, localization-specific, scripting language used to process gender, plurals, conjugations, and most of the other quirky elements of natural language.

The following chapters will show you cool ways L20n solves your localization challenges. Each chapter contains a hands-on example of simple L20n concepts. Begin with chapter one, Hello world.
