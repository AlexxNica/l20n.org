L20n is an open source, localization-specific scripting language used to process gender, plurals, conjugations, and most of the other quirky elements of natural language.

The following chapters will show you cool ways to solve your localization challenges with L20n. Each chapter contains a hands-on example of simple L20n concepts. Begin with Chapter 1. "Hello world".
